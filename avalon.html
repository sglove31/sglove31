Avalon is the 8th World that your young wizard will encounter on their journey. It is a world heavily based on Renaissance-period Europe, evident by the minstrels and bards present within the world. The whimsical breezy vibe adds to the ambience of the world, as well as the extensive amount of detail and variety in each zone.



Avalon does pose some challenges for wizards, as foes tend to have cheats, often requiring a change-up in battle tactics. Though the predominant audience for the game was initially younger children -- typically under the age of 12 -- in 2008, the skill required to successfully complete quests 8 worlds in would require an older audience. Nowadays, the primary audience is typically persons in their 20s-30s, as they play due to the game's nostalgia, and were most-likely children during the game's initial height in gameplay.
